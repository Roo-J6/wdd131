Mountain Explorer Hub - Website Planning Document

Site Name

Mountain Explorer Hub was selected as the site name because it represents a central platform for outdoor enthusiasts who love to explore mountainous regions. It provides a hub of information, resources, and community engagement for hikers, climbers, and nature lovers.

Site Purpose

Mountain Explorer Hub aims to provide detailed information on various mountain trails, gear recommendations, safety tips, and outdoor adventure guides. The website will also serve as a community space where users can share their experiences, photos, and hiking itineraries.

Scenarios

  • What are the best hiking trails for beginners in the Rocky Mountains?
  • Where can I find expert reviews on hiking and climbing gear?

Color Scheme

The chosen colors reflect a natural and adventurous vibe:

#228B22 – Forest Green (Primary)
#8B4513 – Earthy Brown (Secondary)
#F5F5DC – Light Beige (Neutral)

Typography

Headings: Montserrat – clean, modern, and strong presence
Body: Roboto – clean and easy to read for paragraphs and descriptions
Accent: Playfair Display – used for quotes and testimonials

"Every mountain top is within reach if you just keep climbing."

Navigation Structure

  • Home
  • Trail Guides
  • Gear Reviews
  • Community
  • Contact

Wireframe - Mobile View

The homepage will feature a simple vertical layout with a top navigation menu, a featured image, and quick access links to trail guides, gear, and safety tips.

Wireframe - Desktop View

A wider layout with a top navigation bar, a main section for featured articles, and a sidebar for recent posts, user stories, or quick links to gear reviews and guides.

Design and Layout Notes

The design will balance strong visuals with clean layouts for clarity and user experience. The use of earthy tones will make the site feel warm and connected to nature, while clear fonts and accessible navigation will support users on both mobile and desktop devices.
